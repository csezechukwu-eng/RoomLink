# renta bed — Bed/Room Rental Agreement (template skeleton)

> ⚠️ **Not legal advice.** This is a starting skeleton. Have a licensed
> real-estate attorney in your state review and finalize it before use.
> Bed-by-bed rentals are typically structured as **room/bed rental agreements
> with _several_ liability** — each tenant is responsible only for their own
> bed's rent, not the whole unit.

## How to use this with DocuSign
1. Put this content into a document (Google Doc / Word / PDF).
2. In DocuSign: **Templates → New → upload** this document.
3. Add **two roles**: `Landlord` and `Tenant` (set signing order).
4. Add a **Signature** + **Date Signed** tab for each role where indicated.
5. For every `«FieldLabel»` below, add a **Text tab** and set its
   **Data Label** to the exact name shown (case-sensitive). renta bed fills
   these automatically when it sends the lease.
6. Save the template and copy its **Template ID** into `DOCUSIGN_TEMPLATE_ID`.

### Auto-filled field labels (must match exactly)
| Data Label | Filled from renta bed |
|---|---|
| `PropertyName` | Property name |
| `PropertyAddress` | Full property address |

## BED / ROOM RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made between the Landlord and the Tenant identified below for
the rental of a specific bed/room space described herein.

**Premises:** «PropertyName», «PropertyAddress»
**Space rented:** Room «RoomName», Bed «BedLabel»
**Tenant:** «TenantName»

### 1. Term
This Agreement begins on **«LeaseStart»** and ends on **«LeaseEnd»**, unless
otherwise terminated or renewed per the terms below.

### 2. Rent
Tenant agrees to pay **$«MonthlyRent» per month** for the bed space, due on the
date(s) specified by the Landlord. Tenant is responsible only for the rent of
the bed space rented under this Agreement (several liability).

### 3. Security Deposit
Tenant shall pay a security deposit of **$«DepositAmount»**, refundable per the
terms of this Agreement and applicable law.

### 4. Use & House Rules
Tenant shall use the space as a private residence and follow the property's
house rules, quiet hours, guest policy, and common-area expectations.

### 5. Utilities & Services
(Describe what's included — Wi-Fi, water, electricity, laundry, parking, etc.)

### 6. Access
Tenant is granted access to the rented bed space and shared common areas as
described by the Landlord.

### 7. Disclosures
(Insert any required state/local disclosures here. **Federal:** a lead-based
paint disclosure is required for housing built before 1978.)

### 8. Termination
(Notice period, conditions, early termination terms.)

### 9. Entire Agreement
This document is the entire agreement between the parties and may be amended
only in writing signed by both parties.
