## MID-TERM BED RENTAL AGREEMENT (FIXED TERM)

This Mid-Term Bed Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is a license to occupy a
specific bed space for a fixed assignment period, between the Landlord and the
Tenant named below.

- **Premises:** «PropertyName», «PropertyAddress»
- **Space rented:** Room «RoomName», Bed «BedLabel»
- **Tenant:** «TenantName»

### 1. Term — Fixed (30–90 Days)
This Agreement runs for a **fixed term from «LeaseStart» to «LeaseEnd»**
(typically 30–90 days). It ends automatically on the end date unless the parties
agree in writing to extend or convert it to month-to-month.

### 2. Rent
Tenant shall pay **$«MonthlyRent» per month**, prorated for any partial month,
due in advance. Tenant is responsible **only** for the rent of the bed space
rented under this Agreement (several liability).

### 3. Security Deposit
Tenant shall pay a deposit of **$«DepositAmount»**, refundable after the term per
this Agreement and applicable law, less any unpaid rent or damages.

### 4. Use & Occupancy
The bed space is for the Tenant's personal residential use during the assignment.
Tenant agrees to all house rules, quiet hours, guest, and common-area policies.

### 5. Utilities & Services
Wi-Fi, water, electricity, and standard furnishings are included unless the
Landlord states otherwise.

### 6. Access & Common Areas
Tenant may use the rented bed space and designated shared common areas. Landlord
may enter for inspection, repairs, or emergencies with reasonable notice.

### 7. House Rules
The property's posted house rules are incorporated by reference.

### 8. Early Termination
If the Tenant's assignment changes, the Tenant may request early termination with
**at least 14 days' written notice**. Deposit and prorated rent are handled per
this Agreement and applicable law.

### 9. Holdover
If the Tenant stays past «LeaseEnd» without a written extension, occupancy
continues on a 30-day month-to-month basis at the same rate, terminable on 7
days' notice.

### 10. Disclosures
Applicable state/local disclosures apply. **Federal:** for pre-1978 housing, a
lead-based paint disclosure is provided.

### 11. Entire Agreement
This is the entire agreement and may be changed only in a writing signed by both
parties.
