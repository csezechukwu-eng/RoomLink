## SHORT-TERM BED RENTAL AGREEMENT (MONTH-TO-MONTH)

This Short-Term Bed Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is a license to occupy a
specific bed space and is made between the Landlord and the Tenant named below.

- **Premises:** «PropertyName», «PropertyAddress»
- **Space rented:** Room «RoomName», Bed «BedLabel»
- **Tenant:** «TenantName»

### 1. Term — Month-to-Month
This Agreement begins on **«LeaseStart»** and continues on a **30-day,
month-to-month basis**, renewing automatically each 30 days until ended by
either party under Section 8. This is a short-term occupancy and does not create
a long-term tenancy.

### 2. Rent
Tenant shall pay **$«MonthlyRent» per 30-day period**, due in advance on the
renewal date. Tenant is responsible **only** for the rent of the bed space
rented under this Agreement (several liability), not for other occupants.

### 3. Security Deposit
Tenant shall pay a deposit of **$«DepositAmount»**, refundable per this
Agreement and applicable law after deducting unpaid rent or damages.

### 4. Use & Occupancy
The bed space is for the Tenant's personal residential use only. Tenant agrees
to follow all house rules, quiet hours, guest, and common-area policies.

### 5. Utilities & Services
Wi-Fi, water, electricity, and standard furnishings are included unless stated
otherwise by the Landlord.

### 6. Access & Common Areas
Tenant may use the rented bed space and shared common areas as designated.
Landlord may enter for inspection, repairs, or emergencies with reasonable
notice as allowed by law.

### 7. House Rules
Tenant agrees to the property's posted house rules, which are incorporated by
reference and may be updated by the Landlord with reasonable notice.

### 8. Termination
Either party may end this Agreement with **at least 7 days' written notice**
before the next renewal date. Rent already paid for the current period is
non-refundable except as required by law.

### 9. Disclosures
Any state/local disclosures apply. **Federal:** for housing built before 1978, a
lead-based paint disclosure is provided with this Agreement.

### 10. Entire Agreement
This is the entire agreement and may be changed only in a writing signed by both
parties.
