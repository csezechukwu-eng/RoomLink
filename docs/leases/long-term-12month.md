## LONG-TERM BED RENTAL LEASE (12-MONTH TERM)

This Long-Term Bed Rental Lease ("Lease") for a specific bed space is made
between the Landlord and the Tenant named below.

- **Premises:** «PropertyName», «PropertyAddress»
- **Space rented:** Room «RoomName», Bed «BedLabel»
- **Tenant:** «TenantName»

### 1. Term — Fixed 12 Months
This Lease runs for a **fixed term of twelve (12) months, from «LeaseStart» to
«LeaseEnd»**, unless terminated or renewed as provided below.

### 2. Rent
Tenant shall pay **$«MonthlyRent» per month**, due on the 1st of each month.
Tenant is responsible **only** for the rent of the bed space rented under this
Lease (several liability). A late fee may apply to rent not paid when due.

### 3. Security Deposit
Tenant shall pay a deposit of **$«DepositAmount»**, held and refunded per this
Lease and applicable state law, less any unpaid rent, fees, or damages beyond
normal wear and tear.

### 4. Use & Occupancy
The bed space is for the Tenant's personal residential use as their primary
residence. Tenant agrees to all house rules, quiet hours, guest, and common-area
policies.

### 5. Utilities & Services
Wi-Fi, water, electricity, and standard furnishings are included unless the
Landlord states otherwise in writing.

### 6. Maintenance
Tenant shall keep the bed space and shared areas clean and promptly report
maintenance issues. Tenant is responsible for damage they or their guests cause.

### 7. Access
Landlord may enter the bed space and common areas for inspection, repairs, or
emergencies with reasonable notice as required by law.

### 8. House Rules
The property's posted house rules are incorporated into this Lease by reference.

### 9. Renewal & Holdover
At the end of the term, this Lease may renew by written agreement. If the Tenant
remains without a renewal, the tenancy continues month-to-month at the same rate,
terminable on 30 days' written notice by either party.

### 10. Early Termination
Early termination requires **at least 30 days' written notice** and is subject to
the terms of this Lease and applicable law.

### 11. Disclosures
Applicable state/local disclosures apply. **Federal:** for housing built before
1978, a lead-based paint disclosure is provided with this Lease.

### 12. Entire Agreement
This Lease is the entire agreement between the parties and may be amended only in
a writing signed by both parties.
